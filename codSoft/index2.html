Traditional Gujarati Delicacy

Undhiyu is a hearty and flavorful Gujarati mixed vegetable dish, typically prepared in winter. It features a medley of vegetables like purple yam, potatoes, green beans, and eggplant, cooked with a blend of spices. The unique flavor comes from fenugreek dumplings (methi muthiyas), adding texture. Slow-cooked in an earthen pot, the spices meld beautifully with the vegetables, creating a rich, aromatic dish. Often enjoyed with puris or rotis, Undhiyu is a staple at festive occasions in Gujarat.
